Navigation

Mobile app nav is exactly Feed, Market, Messages, Orders, Profile.

Market

Use Mint. Use Sell, Trade, Looking For, Free / Donation. Do not use Add to Cart as the dominant action.

Orders

Use pending_payment, paid, cancelled, shipped, refunded, completed. Hide invalid buyer/seller actions.

Neutral
